Practical guide of the temperature switch between nitrogen and helium¶

Danger

N2 in all conditions should be above 77K (N2 --> all temperature-->77K)

Cooling temperature of cryocooler with N2 : room temperature 300K --> 95K

  • Step 1 : Open the N2 valve and close He valve
  • Step 2 : FOURC > prepnitrogen (# it will setup the temperature in the Lakeshore controller)
  • Step 3 : FOURC > te 170 (# lowering temperature to 170K)

Cooling temperature of cryocooler with He : 77K --> 13K (change to N2 to He flow)

  • Step 1 : Please let CHESS operator know about changing the gas flow from N2 to He
  • Step 2 : FOURC > te 180 (# it will setup the temperature in the Lakeshore controller)
  • Step 3 : Turn on the He valve and close N2 valve together
  • Step 4 : FOURC > runninghelium (# it will setup the temperature in the Lakeshore controller)
  • Step 5 : Wait for base temperature to stabilize
  • Step 6 : FOURC > te 13 (# lowering temperature to 13K or others)

Please use Helium sensibly!

Heating temperature of cryocooler : 13K --> 78K (change to He to N2 flow)

  • Step 1 : FOURC > prepnitrogen (# it will setup the temperature in the Lakeshore controller)
  • Step 2 : FOURC > te 180 (# it will setup the temperature in the Lakeshore controller)
  • Step 2 : Make sure all the temperature is above 77 K (channel A, B and C in PLD controller)
  • Step 3 : Open the N2 valve and close He valve together
  • Step 4 : FOURC > te 100 (# increase temperature to 100K or any other desired temperature)

Performing exoeriment above room temperature : 300K --> 500K

  • Step 1 : Make sure N2 valve is open and He is closed
  • Step 2: FOURC > prephighT (# increase temperature to 1st and 2nd controller to 143K and 220K)
  • Step 3 : Make sure in the controller 1 (channel 1) and 2 (channel 3), it will to reach the temperature.
  • Step 4 : FOURC > te 400 (# increase temperature to 400K)

Goging to room temperature

  • Step 1 : Heat the temperature 300K, 300K, 300K
  • Step 2 : close the compressor
  • Step 3 : Wait until go to the required temperature
  • Step 4 : Close the valve
